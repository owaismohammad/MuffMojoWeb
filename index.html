Sprinkle Some Fun on Your Chats

Summon your favorite emojis while having a chat just by typing their names. Skip the mouse, and forget about the clicks. Just type the name of an emoji, and make it instantly yours to use.

How It Works?

  • The extension will detect words and show related emojis.
  • Type the name of the emoji you want, and it’ll appear as a tooltip.
  • Press the Up Arrow key to select your emoji.
  • All the emojis a word AWAY! 😀 😁 😎 ❤️ 💥 🎉 🥳 🌟
  • Available for Whatsapp. Stay Tuned for Future Iterations!
